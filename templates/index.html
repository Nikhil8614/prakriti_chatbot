About Ayurveda and Tridosha

Ayurveda is a traditional system of medicine that originated in India thousands of years ago. It emphasizes the balance of three fundamental energies known as Tridosha: Vata, Pitta, and Kapha.

Vata represents the elements of air and ether and is associated with movement. Pitta represents the elements of fire and water and is linked to digestion and metabolism. Kapha embodies the elements of water and earth and is responsible for stability and structure.

An individual's unique Prakruti is determined by the dominance of one or more of these doshas. Understanding your Prakruti can help you make informed choices about diet, lifestyle, and healthcare.

Why Prakruti Assessment?

Ayurveda believes in individualized treatment and diet plans. Discovering your unique Prakruti can help you prevent diseases and maintain overall well-being.
